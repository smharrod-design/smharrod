Hiya, I'm Sean, a UX & Product Designer with 10+ years’ experience across digital, print, and product design. Based in Wirral.

I’ve delivered design work for brands like British Red Cross, Oxfam, CRUK, and Peacocks. I currently work at Cybertill, designing new product features and eCommerce experiences.

My background spans print, branding, UX, and motion. I love simplifying complexity, collaborating with developers, and designing with real-world constraints in mind.

When I’m not working I’m probably watching or playing footy, 3d printing, or obsessing over a new cooking project.
